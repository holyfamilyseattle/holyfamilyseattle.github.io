LFML encourages everyone, especially the CCD students, to attend this fun event.

  3. Christmas Crib Competition: “Gloria in Excelsis” - Crib Making Family Video Contest - Due December 29, 2022. The diocesan LFML is organizing a Christmas crib contest, named “Gloria in Excelsis”, throughout the diocese. This is a video contest for families. Sunday school students in grades 4 through 12 from the parishes or missions of the St Thomas Syro-Malabar Catholic Diocese of Chicago are eligible to attend. Each family can make a Christmas crib inside or on the grounds of their home and film the process. The best three videos from each Zone will be chosen and considered for diocesan-level selection. The winners will be honored at the diocesan level. Please see the attached document for more information.

        The final edited video can be submitted to the diocesan team by using the google form
